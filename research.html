Research Interests

研究方向

Integrated photonic chips

集成光子芯片

Development of photonic integrated circuits (PICs) and photonic crystal surface emitting lasers (PCSELs) for optical information and computing applications.

研发光子集成回路（PIC）与光子晶体面发射激光器（PCSEL），应用于光学信息与计算领域。

Quantum light-matter hybrids

光-物质强耦合宏观量子态

Investigation of strong light-matter interactions in novel van der Waals materials, realization of their macroscopic quantum phases and collective excitations, and exploration of their applications in optoelectronic chips.

研究新型范德华材料中的强光-物质相互作用，实现其宏观量子相和集体激发，探索其在光电芯片领域的应用。

Nano-electro-mechanics

纳米机电系统

Development of advanced nano-electro-mechanical systems (NEMS) for radio-frequency signal processing and quantum phononics.

研发新型的纳米机电系统（NEMS），应用于射频信号处理与量子声子学领域。
