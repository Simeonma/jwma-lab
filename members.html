Dr. Jingwen Ma (马静文)

Associate Professor (Incoming)
准聘副教授（即将入职）
Dr. Jingwen Ma received his Ph.D. degree in Electronic Engineering from The Chinese University of Hong Kong in 2021. He is currently a Research Assistant Professor at the University of Hong Kong, and will join the Department of Electrical and Electronic Engineering, Southern University of Science and Technology in June 2026. His research interests focus on integrated photonic devices, light-matter interaction physics, and metamaterials.
马静文博士于2021年获得香港中文大学电子工程博士学位，现任香港大学研究助理教授，将于2026年6月加入南方科技大学电子与电气工程系。研究方向包括集成光子器件、光-物质相互作用物理及纳米超材料。
