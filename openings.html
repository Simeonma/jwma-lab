Lab Openings

We always welcome passionate and motivated candidates interested in our lab's work to contact us about potential positions. Interested candidates are encouraged to email the PI with CV, description of past research experiences and future research interests to inquire research opportunities in the lab.

Open Positions

Postdoctoral Fellow
Research directions: topological photonics, integrated photonic devices, metamaterials/nanophotonics, optoelectronic information. Responsible for independent research project design, experimental implementation and paper publication, and participate in the application of scientific research projects with the research team.
Postgraduate Student
Research directions: topological photonics, silicon-based integrated photonics, photonic metamaterials, low-dimensional material optoelectronic devices. Undertake the research tasks of the laboratory's scientific research projects, complete the experimental testing and data analysis, and publish high-level academic papers on schedule.
Research Assistant / Undergraduate Intern
Responsible for assisting researchers in experimental operation, sample preparation, equipment maintenance and data sorting. Those with basic knowledge of optics, electronics or materials science are preferred; interns can obtain academic guidance and experimental training opportunities.
