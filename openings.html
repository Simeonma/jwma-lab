About us

Our research group explores cutting-edge interdisciplinary topics at the intersection of nano-device engineering and photonic physics, including integrated photonic chips, semiconductor lasers, nonlinear optics, van der Waals materials, nanomechanics, exciton physics, and metamaterials.

In our lab, we strive to build a welcoming, supportive community where everyone can fully embrace the pure joy of scientific research, and grow into the best version of themselves. If you are passionate about pushing the boundaries of optoelectronic engineering and physics, and this vision resonates with you, we warmly welcome you to apply!
