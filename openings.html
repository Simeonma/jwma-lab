About Us

关于我们

Our research group is interested in cutting-edge topics at the intersection of nano-device engineering and photonic physics, including but not limited to silicon/lithium niobate/lithium tantalate integrated photonic chips, III-V semiconductor lasers, nonlinear optics, van der Waals materials physics, nano-optomechanics and nano-acoustics, topological physics, exciton physics, Bose-Einstein condensates, and metamaterials.

We believe that the essence of scientific research lies in pure exploration and creation — thus, we are committed to building an inclusive and supportive team environment. We hope every member can enjoy the joy of exploring the unknown, unleash creativity in an open and free atmosphere, and eventually grow into an independent and competent researcher . If you are interested in our research, we welcome you to reach out!
我们对纳米器件工程与光子物理交叉领域的许多前沿课题都感兴趣，包括但不限于硅 / 铌酸锂 / 钽酸锂集成光子芯片、三五族半导体激光器、非线性光学、范德华材料物理、纳米光力学和纳米声学、拓扑物理、激子物理、玻色爱因斯坦凝聚、超材料等方向。

我们坚信科研的本质是纯粹的探索与创造 —— 因此我们致力于维持一个包容互助的团队环境。我们希望让每一位成员都能纯粹地享受探索未知的乐趣，在开放自由的氛围中释放创造力，最终成长为独当一面的科研工作者。如果你对我们研究感兴趣，欢迎联系我们！
