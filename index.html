集成光子学与量子光学创新研究 Innovative Research in Integrated & Quantum Photonics

聚焦拓扑光子学、光-物质相互作用、片上声子学与宏观量子态等前沿方向，致力于探索光子器件的基础物理与集成应用，推动新一代光量子技术的发展。 Focus on frontier fields such as topological photonics, light-matter interaction, on-chip phononics and macroscopic quantum states, dedicated to exploring the fundamental physics and integrated applications of photonic devices, and promoting the development of next-generation photonic quantum technologies.
