We are an interdisciplinary research group in photonic chips and macroscopic quantum phase, with expertise in optical and electrical measurements, nano-fabrications, and theoretical modeling. Application-wise, we develop advanced photonic devices for on-chip information and computing applications, for instance silicon photonic chips, III-V semiconductor lasers, and opto-electro-mechanical chips. Scientifically, we investigate quantum phases of light-matter hybrids and their exotic properties, such as Bose-Einstein condensations, quantum fluids and solids.

我们从事光子芯片与宏观光量子物态的交叉科学研究，研究内容涵盖光学与电学测量、纳米加工、理论建模等方向。应用层面，我们研发面向光信息与光计算领域的先进光子器件及芯片，包括硅光集成芯片、III-V 族半导体激光器、光机电芯片等；科学层面，我们聚焦光 - 物质强耦合体系的宏观量子相及其奇异特性研究，致力于在芯片上实现玻色 - 爱因斯坦凝聚、量子流体与量子固体等宏观量子效应。

Strong light-matter interactions: We report the first excitonic negative refraction mediated by magnetic orders. This work is published in Nature Nanotechnology.
Nano-electro-mechanical chips: We report the first topological nano-electro-mechanical chip with auxiliary orbital degree of freedom. This work is published in Nature Nanotechnology.
III-V semiconductor lasers: We demonstrate photonic-crystal micro-cavity lasers with Dirac-vortex states. This work is published in Light: Science & Applications.
Metamaterials: We experimentally realize vortex-string mode in metamaterials. This work is published in Nature Communications.
Photonic integrated circuits: We experimentally realize photonic integrated circuits using valley kink states. This work is published in Laser Photonics Review.
