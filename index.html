We are an interdisciplinary research group in photonic chips and macroscopic quantum phase, with expertise in optical and electrical measurements, nano-fabrications, and theoretical modeling. Application-wise, we develop advanced photonic devices for on-chip information and computing applications, for instance silicon photonic chips, III-V semiconductor lasers, and opto-electro-mechanical chips. Scientifically, we investigate quantum phases of light-matter hybrids and their exotic properties, such as Bose-Einstein condensations, quantum fluids and solids.

我们从事光子芯片与宏观量子物态的交叉科学研究，研究内容涵盖光学与电学测量、纳米加工、理论建模等方向。应用层面，我们研发面向光信息与光计算领域的先进光子器件及芯片，包括硅光集成芯片、III-V 族半导体激光器、光机电芯片等；科学层面，我们聚焦光 - 物质强耦合体系的宏观量子相及其奇异特性研究，致力于在芯片上实现玻色 - 爱因斯坦凝聚、量子流体与量子固体等宏观量子效应。

Research Interests

研究方向

Integrated photonic chips

集成光子芯片

Development of photonic integrated circuits (PICs) and photonic crystal surface emitting lasers (PCSELs) for optical information and computing applications.

研发光子集成回路（PIC）与光子晶体面发射激光器（PCSEL），应用于光学信息与计算领域。

Quantum light-matter hybrids

光-物质强耦合宏观量子态

Investigation of strong light-matter interactions in novel van der Waals materials, realization of their macroscopic quantum phases and collective excitations, and exploration of their applications in optoelectronic chips.

研究新型范德华材料中的强光-物质相互作用，实现其宏观量子相和集体激发，探索其在光电芯片领域的应用。

Nano-electro-mechanics

纳米机电系统

Development of advanced nano-electro-mechanical systems (NEMS) for radio-frequency signal processing and quantum phononics.

研发新型的纳米机电系统（NEMS），应用于射频信号处理与量子声子学领域。
