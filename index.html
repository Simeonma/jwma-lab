We merge quantum physics with photonic nano-structures and chips: Application-wise, we develop high-performance photonic computing and quantum information systems; Scientifically, we investigate exotic properties of macroscopic quantum coherent phases, such as Bose-Einstein condensation and supersolids, under ambient conditions.

Research Directions

1. Integrated Photonics & Semiconductor Lasers

Develop on-chip integrated photonic devices, topological lasers and 2D material-based heterogeneous optoelectronic devices for photonic computing, realizing high performance, miniaturization and on-chip integration.

2. Light-Matter Interaction for Microscopic Quantum Coherent States

Investigate light-matter coupling in low-dimensional/topological systems, exploring the evolution of microscopic quantum coherent states such as BEC, superfluidity and topological states of light.

3. Quantum Photonics

Based on quantum control of light-matter interaction, develop topological polaritonic devices, high-performance quantum light sources and photonic quantum state information processing systems.
