Lab Facilities & Equipment

Photonics Test Systems

High-Resolution Optical Spectrum Analyzer
Covers the spectral range of 600-1700 nm with a resolution of 0.01 nm, suitable for characterizing the emission spectrum of photonic devices, laser performance and optical signal analysis.
Laser Scanning Confocal Microscope
High-precision optical imaging system with a spatial resolution of ≤0.2 μm, used for micro-nano structure characterization, photonic device morphology observation and in-situ optical performance testing.
Femtosecond Laser Processing System
1030 nm femtosecond laser with pulse width <200 fs, supports high-precision micro-nano processing of photonic crystals, metamaterials and integrated photonic device structures.

Integrated Photonic Device Fabrication Platform

High-Speed Spin Coater
Speed range 500-10000 rpm, with precise speed control, suitable for uniform coating of photoresist on silicon, glass and 2D material substrates for micro-nano patterning.
Inductively Coupled Plasma (ICP) Etcher
High-precision dry etching equipment, supports etching of silicon, silicon dioxide, nitride and other materials, with an etching uniformity of ±3% for photonic device structure fabrication.
High-Frequency Probe Station
Equipped with optical fiber coupling probe and RF probe, supports on-wafer testing of integrated photonic devices, including insertion loss, extinction ratio and high-speed modulation performance.

Topological Photonics & Metamaterials Characterization Platform

Microwave Vector Network Analyzer
Frequency range 100 kHz-40 GHz, used for characterizing the transmission, reflection and topological edge state properties of microwave metamaterials and photonic topological structures.
Near-Field Scanning Optical Microscope (NSOM)
Spatial resolution <50 nm, realizes sub-wavelength optical near-field imaging, suitable for characterizing the local optical field distribution of topological photonic devices and metamaterials.
