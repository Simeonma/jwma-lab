Lab Facilities & Equipment

Photonics Test Systems

Transient pump-probe setup
Ti:sapphire amplifier (Asterella fs laser, wavelength @ 800 nm, 1 kHz repetition rate), optical parametric amplifier (OPerA Solo, wavelength @ 400–1100 nm), delay stage (delay time 2.5 ns), lock-in amplifier (SR830).
Home-built angle-resolved microscopy
Home-built microscope, monochromator (Shamrock 750), single-photon detector (Becker&Hickl PMC-100-4), CCD (Andor Newton, iStar sCMOS), Cryostat (C04-005-003), pico-second laser diode (Becker&Hickl C200-HD15, wavelength @ 385 nm), single-photon counters (IDQ), piezo-stages (CONEX-AG).
Near-infrared microscopy
Pump lasers (high power ns laser), infrared spectrometer (Andor Kymera 3281-B2 + iDus CCD), infrared CMOS camera (Hamamusu), Objectives (Olympus 50x IR).
