Principal Investigator

Dr. Jingwen Ma

马静文博士

Associate Professor (Incoming)
准聘副教授（即将入职）
Dr. Jingwen Ma received his Ph.D. degree in Electronic Engineering from The Chinese University of Hong Kong in 2021. He is currently a Research Assistant Professor in Prof. Xiang Zhang's group at the University of Hong Kong, and will join the Department of Electrical and Electronic Engineering, Southern University of Science and Technology in June 2026. His research interests include integrated photonic chips, semiconductor lasers, light-matter interaction physics, and metamaterials.
2021年获得香港中文大学电子工程博士学位，现在香港大学张翔院士课题组任研究助理教授，将于2026年6月加入南方科技大学电子与电气工程系。研究方向包括集成光子芯片、半导体激光器、光-物质相互作用物理、纳米超表面与超材料等。
